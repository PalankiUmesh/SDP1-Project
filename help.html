BidOps Guide

  • What is bidding?

    Bidding is an offer to set a price tag by an individual or business for a product or service or a demand that something be done. Bidding is used to determine the cost or value of some products. Online Bidding means an electronic procurement process in which agencies receive bids from vendors for goods, services, or construction documents over the Internet or an web application.

    Bidding Process

    The bidding process involves 5 step:

    Bid Solicitation: This is where the project manager request for proposals.

    Bid Submission: This step should include all the documentary of the product.

    Bid Selection: All the vendors starts their bidding prices here in this step.

    Contract Formation: After the owner selects a bid that meets their requirements, the parties involved must form and sign a legally binding contract beforeÂ pre-construction. This is also an opportunity for final pricing negotiations by the company that wins the bid.

    Project Delivery: After forming the contract, the project is ready to roll based on the project delivery method used.

    Implementation

    BidOps is an online bidding application. This application allows to sell the products, projects or buy the products through online. The manager upload the documents and information regarding the project into BitOps, the bidders randomly browse the product and set their bids. Manager calculates all the analysis of the bids and then deliver it to the highest bidder and accurate vendor.
